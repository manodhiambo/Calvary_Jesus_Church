CALVARY JESUS CHURCH



Welcome to Calvary Jesus Church website. We are a group of people with a common faith, belief that God can never forgive sin without a sacrifice. Due to this, He sent His Son Jesus Christ as a sacrifice, punished him on the cross on behalf of the believers. By the death of His son, all believers' sins are forgiven(Acts 13:39-39) and they are therefore saved from the wrath of God which is Hell(Romans 5:9, 1st Thessalonians 1:10) We Believe among other Scripturial that none can be right with God by keeping the law(Galatians 2:16) since if you break one command, you become guilty of all. This leaves us with no any other option but to put our trust in Jesus Christ as the living sacrifice, without which there's no forgiveness of sin, no God and subsiquently hell awaits . Welcome to our Sunday services as from 9:00AM to 11:00AM .

We are currently located at Nyaduong' village next to Nyaduong' Secondary and Primary School. Our aim is to direct you to God through His Son Jesus Christ. This is only possible through sharing the true gospel with you; the gospel of Jesus Christ. Believing the gospel and agreeing with God that you deserve Hell (Repentance) and getting saved from the wrath of God (Hell) Romans 5:9, 1st Thessalonians 1:10 through forgiveness
