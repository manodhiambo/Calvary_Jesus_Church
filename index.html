Welcome to Calvary Jesus Church

We are a group of people with a common faith, believing that God cannot forgive sin without a sacrifice. Therefore, He sent His Son, Jesus Christ, as a sacrifice, punished on the cross for believers. By His death, all believers' sins are forgiven (Acts 13:38-39) and they are saved from God's wrath (Romans 5:9, 1 Thessalonians 1:10).

We believe none can be right with God by keeping the law (Galatians 2:16). Trust in Jesus Christ is the only way to forgiveness, God, and salvation.
